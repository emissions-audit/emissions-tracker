Why This Exists

~100 companies produce ~71% of global emissions. The data to hold them accountable exists but is scattered across PDFs, inconsistent formats, and paywalled databases costing $25K–$200K/year.

Cross-Validation Engine

SEC filings say one thing. Climate TRACE satellites say another. We normalize both, compare them, and flag discrepancies with red/yellow/green severity.

4 Independent Sources

SEC EDGAR (regulatory filings), Climate TRACE (satellite measurement), CDP (voluntary disclosure), sustainability report PDFs — all normalized into one schema.

Free API, Forever

REST API with Swagger docs. 100 req/min free tier for researchers, journalists, and developers. Raw data dumps on GitHub.

Open Source & Auditable

MIT licensed. Every normalization decision and discrepancy algorithm is in the code. Fork it, self-host it, challenge our methodology.

Try the API

List energy companies
curl https://api.emissions-tracker.org/v1/companies?sector=energy
Find major discrepancies between reported and measured emissions
curl https://api.emissions-tracker.org/v1/discrepancies?flag=red
Compare companies side-by-side
curl "https://api.emissions-tracker.org/v1/emissions/compare?companies=ID1,ID2&scopes=1,2&years=2022,2023"
Download full dataset
curl -H "X-API-Key: YOUR_KEY" https://api.emissions-tracker.org/v1/export/full?format=csv
